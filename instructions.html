Welcome to the cutting edge space rover program. In our control room, we can position our Mars rover on its Plateau by moving it along a grid.

The rover moves by sending Move ("M") commands the rover moving it one unit on the grid, or Rotate ("L","R") commands to rotate the rover 90deg in either direction. It can take as many commands as you enter. (Ex. "M, L, M, M, R, M, M")
